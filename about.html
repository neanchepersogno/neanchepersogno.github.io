Neanche Per Sogno

Neanche Per Sogno is a curatorial project active in the field of electronic and electroacoustic music. Founded by Martenot in 2022, it collaborates with artists through releases, mixtapes, and concerts, exploring experimental modes of sound production and consumption while integrating editorial, visual, and performative disciplines in an open and non-linear perspective. It operates across artistic disciplines and is based on an approach where active listening—understood as a primary compositional process—and sound research intertwine with DIY practices.
