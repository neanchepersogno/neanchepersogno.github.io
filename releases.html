Guglielmo Diana / Lefka – Split

Despite not knowing each other before, electroacoustic composer Guglielmo Diana and multidisciplinary artist Gustavo Cambareri, aka Lefka, join forces and nicely complement one another via skewed electronics in the third release curated by Neanche Per Sogno.

NXS003 opens with two tracks composed by Guglielmo Diana, who masterfully creates two magmatic distillations extracted from a maximalist sound palette of field recordings and hard digital processing of concrete samples. Through exhausting micro agglomerations, he forges frenetic visceral narratives that dynamically traverse various emotional states.

The arrival of Lefka and his distinctive multi-layering oversaturation turns out to be the emotional turning point where two dazzling streams of light, originating from the same sound source, converge blindingly in full spectrum toward the listener's eyes.

Released May 24, 2024

Martenot - Faceshopping RMX
Originally composed as an acousmatic composition in an extended version for eight channels between 2022 and 2023 during the electroacoustic composition class taught by Professor Stefano Bassanese at the G. Verdi Conservatory of Turin, Faceshopping RMX stemmed from the desire to pay homage to the British producer SOPHIE, a musician who has greatly influenced and inspired Martenot in recent years. The piece consists of a composition that oscillates between a remix and an expanded rework of Faceshopping.

This work was developed by starting with a matryoshka segmentation of the spectrum of the original track, dividing it vertically, horizontally, or in a hybrid manner into subsections, phrases, fragments, and relevant gestural micro-fragments. Subsequently, after a phase of reverse engineering, numerous related versions were created - at different degrees of proximity - synthesized from scratch using FM and Pulsar synthesis systems, primarily using Supercollider, as well as wavetable synthesis using the Serum plugin. Finally, a significant portion of the obtained signal was manipulated using the Waveset Distortion system, developed in Max/MSP by Marco Marasciuolo, expanding the expressive capacity of each of them in relation to an augmented reformulation of each articulation.

Released August 26, 2023

Martenot - Sfilate Spaziali
Italian composer Martenot and founder of Neanche Per Sogno unleashes his debut EP called Sfilate Spaziali, which consists of three pieces entirely based on minimal vocabulary and instrumental apparatus. Aiming to exploit the entire musical spectrum, they orbit around a solid sound body, which has as its main source a monophonic synthesizer that he developed in Max/MSP e dà una precisa direzione timbrica con vincoli estetici radicali. Ogni traccia dell’EP si distingue dalle altre sia per il concept che per l’architettura compositiva. Ogni pezzo, infatti, ha una forma propria, derivante dal polimorfismo dei processi, dall’incrocio degli stessi e dalla ricerca di un uso sempre nuovo del materiale sonoro iniziale. Tuttavia, la caratteristica che unisce le tre Sfilate Spaziali è l’alternanza dicotomica di momenti ritmicamente serrati in contrasto con parti più rarefatte e ariose. Nelle prime, il materiale digitale è compresso in uno schema ritmico solido, quasi claustrofobico, che evoca le strutture molecolari rigide dei materiali plastici e che non lascia spazio all’azione se non la ripetizione delle varie componenti. Nelle sezioni più libere e irregolari, invece, il tessuto musicale si dissolve e si apre a scenari più ampi. Questa successione continua di ritmi densi ed espansioni sonore dà vita alle Sfilate Spaziali, infine integrate dai singoli titoli delle tracce, che contribuiscono pienamente alla loro esistenza.

Released September 11, 2022
