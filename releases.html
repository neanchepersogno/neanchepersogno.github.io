Guglielmo Diana / Lefka – Split

Despite not knowing each other before, electroacoustic composer Guglielmo Diana and multidisciplinary artist Gustavo Cambareri, aka Lefka, join forces and nicely complement one another via skewed electronics in the third release curated by Neanche Per Sogno.

NXS003 opens with two tracks composed by Guglielmo Diana, who masterfully creates two magmatic distillations extracted from a maximalist sound palette of field recordings and hard digital processing of concrete samples. Through exhausting micro agglomerations, he forges frenetic visceral narratives that dynamically traverse various emotional states.

The arrival of Lefka and his distinctive multi-layering oversaturation turns out to be the emotional turning point where two dazzling streams of light, originating from the same sound source, converge blindingly in full spectrum toward the listener's eyes.

Martenot - Faceshopping RMX

Originally composed as an acousmatic composition in an extended version for eight channels between 2022 and 2023 during the electroacoustic composition class taught by Professor Stefano Bassanese at the G. Verdi Conservatory of Turin, Faceshopping RMX stemmed from the desire to pay homage to the British producer SOPHIE, a musician who has greatly influenced and inspired Martenot in recent years. The piece consists of a composition that oscillates between a remix and an expanded rework of Faceshopping.

This work was developed by starting with a matryoshka segmentation of the spectrum of the original track, dividing it into subsections, phrases, fragments, and relevant gestural micro-fragments. These were then re-synthesized from scratch using FM, Pulsar synthesis (via Supercollider), and wavetable synthesis (via Serum). Waveset Distortion (developed in Max/MSP by Marco Marasciuolo) was also employed to expand expressive possibilities.

The final piece was surgically assembled to create a formal architecture faithful to the original, yet amplified and reimagined in tone, structure, and gesture—highlighting Martenot’s fascination with contrast, hyperkinetic form, and synthetic dreamlike qualities.

Martenot - Sfilate Spaziali

Italian composer Martenot, founder of Neanche Per Sogno Records, debuts with Sfilate Spaziali, an EP of three tracks based on minimal vocabulary and instrument design. At the core is a monophonic synthesizer developed in Max/MSP, providing a distinctive timbral identity under radical aesthetic constraints.

Each track features a unique formal structure born from polymorphic processes and diverse manipulation of core material. The unifying thread is the alternation of tightly rhythmic sections—evoking claustrophobic, plastic rigidity—and airy, rarefied interludes where sound expands and breathes.
